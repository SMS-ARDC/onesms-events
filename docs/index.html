About the Benefit Concert Show

Sisters of Mary has been nurturing and empowering children since 1964. The first Philippine campus opened in 1985, beginning a mission of hope, care, and education for the underprivileged. This benefit concert celebrates 40 years of transforming lives through your support and generosity.

Program Highlights

Folk Dances of Luzon: Senior students showcase timeless traditional routines from Luzon.
Choir & Jasmine Dance: The choir performs beloved classics as dancers bring stories to life through graceful movements.
Regional Folk Segments: Vibrant performances featuring Visayan pandereatas and the iconic Mindanao singkil.
Alumni Tribute: A heartfelt video message and live performance from our alumni community.
